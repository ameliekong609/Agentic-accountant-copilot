# Product Vision

Agentic Accountant Copilot prepares review-ready financial statement workpapers from client source documents while keeping accountants in control of professional judgment.

## Positioning

This is a copilot, not an autopilot. The product should reduce manual work and improve traceability, but it must not silently approve accounting judgments.

## Core promise

- Plan the engagement automatically.
- Delegate work to specialist agents.
- Preserve source evidence for numbers.
- Maintain a central exception queue.
- Ask accountants only for judgment or risk decisions.
- Learn approved preferences and reuse them safely.
- Block final output when critical controls are unresolved.

## Success metric

An accountant can move from source documents to a review-ready workpaper pack materially faster, with a clearer audit trail and fewer repeated preference decisions.
